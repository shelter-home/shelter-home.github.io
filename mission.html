Our Mission And Goals

1) To rescue and protect children from the trauma of physical and sexual abuse, extreme neglect, being abandoned and being a victim or witness of domestic violence.

2) To help the children overcome their initial trauma and restore self-acceptance, dignity and their trust in humanity by giving them unconditional love, care, and an education.

3) To unite the children with their families where possible.

4) By frequent follow-up to improve the above so that they can become useful residents.


Childhood At Risk

We primarily work among children and teenagers who are abused, abandoned, neglected or at risk. That's our FOCUS.


Childhood & Future

We aim to ensure the consistency and happiness of life that all children deserve and to ensure a good vision for their future. This is our Mission.


Protecting

We offer protection by engaging in the rescue and protection of children from unsafe and hazardous environments/situations.


Guiding

We want to develop our children into well balanced, mature and positive members of society.


Healing

We aim to rehabilitate children from their past hurts in our custody and to help them heal the wounds and pain of neglect and to guarantee that their past encounters do not pose a challenge and a danger to their potential undertakings.


Loving Families & Strong Communities

We believe that we are proactive in creating social literacy campaigns and community-based initiatives, and that we are a reliable force in promoting meaningful cultural transformation and improved governance. Working bwyond homes.
